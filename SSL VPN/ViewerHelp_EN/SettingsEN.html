4 Settings
Click Settings and check/uncheck Auto Connect or Auto Reconnect and click OK to enable/disable these functions.
Auto Connect—Automatically uses the connection chosen last time when starting.
Auto Reconnect—Automatically re-establishes a connection to ensure connection stability in the following situations:
Connection times out.
The current user is forced offline by the administrator of an SSL VPN server.
The SSL VPN tunnel fails.
SSL VPN Client will not initiate a reconnection in the following situations:
Negotiation between SSL VPN Client and the server fails.
The server is unreachable or cannot provide SSL VPN services.
The network is unavailable.
Users disconnect connections manually.
